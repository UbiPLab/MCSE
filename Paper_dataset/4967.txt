AB An intelligent streetlight management stem based on LED lamps, designed to facilitate its deployment in existing facilities. As street lights and traffic signals are crucial part of our day to day life this research focuses on two important aspects of this. First one is An Intelligent streetlight management system based on LED lamps which provides a smart and intelligent system for saving of nonrenewable sources of energy. Second important aspect implemented in this research work deals with the flow of traffic and the pollution levels at various places by monitoring the fundamentals of the air quality controls.

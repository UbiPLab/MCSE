DE Cloud computing; Freenas; Open Source; computer networks; Availability
AB The use of cloud services is a reality widely adopted by large organizations and small users. These services provide ease, agility and scalability. Therefore, this research aims to demonstrate the feasibility of creating and using a private cloud at low cost, providing quality and security, using modest hardware. In the research conducted the best evaluated open source software was Freenas, which in a set of plugins provides among other services, storage and local and cloud sharing, and this is a viable option for small and medium enterprises, using their obsolete hardware to expanding or creating storage and sharing on the local network, and or, private cloud. Demonstrating the forms of management and monitoring, demonstrating the configuration of permissions, user activities, availability and registration of activities developed in a configured environment, making it possible to view by individual user or group of users.

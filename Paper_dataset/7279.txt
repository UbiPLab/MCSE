DE cloud computing platform; monitoring system; cloud services
AB Cloud computing platform has the characteristics of virtuality, hierarchical and dynamic, which is more complicated than grid computing. Cloud computing is one of the most popular research topics in the computer field, and the monitoring technology in the cloud environment is attracting more and more attention. As an important part of cloud computing, cloud environment monitoring also plays a vital role in cloud computing. In the research, the research status quo of the subject was investigated, the related theory and technology were analyzed, the cloud computing platform monitoring system was designed, the system was carried on the example experiment, the feasibility and advanced of the system were explained with concrete data.

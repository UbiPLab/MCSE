AB Economic globalization is one of the most important characteristics of the contemporary world economic development. As the inevitable result of the development of human economy, economic globalization can promote the progress of human economic society. Under the background of economic globalization, e-commerce in China has been developed and developed, and has presented a new trend of development. On the basis of the current situation of the development of e-commerce in China, the innovation mode of e-commerce was studied in this paper. Through cloud computing, mobile Internet and the Internet of things, the impacts of the development of e-commerce were analyzed. And then the innovation strategy of e-commerce in China was explored in the system, e-commerce technology and cross-border electricity supplier, etc.

DE data mining; teachers in colleges and universities; work management
AB The current society is a society of information. The rapid development of internet also makes other industries develop rapidly. In the wake of the coming of the era of big data and cloud computing, every industry can work with the help of data mining technology. Therefore, the dependency of people on the data mining is becoming larger and larger. This paper mainly studies the teacher work management system on data mining technology, which aims to provide the vast teachers with a convenient and simple work mode so as to reduce the teachers' work pressure. This paper firstly introduces the relevant study situations and then relevantly analyses the work management system designed mainly for teachers in colleges and universities.

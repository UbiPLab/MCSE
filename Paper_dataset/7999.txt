DE cloud computing; teaching platform; English major; teaching research
AB Cloud computing aided teaching platform is equipped with strong collaborative and supportive functions and has been widely accepted and adopted by teaching staff since it has been introduced into education research area. In English major collaborative learning teaching, cloud computing aided teaching platform has greatly prompted development of English major students' collaborative learning teaching through realization of multi-people collaboration. Through elaborating concepts of cloud computing aided teaching platform and its teaching functional modules, the paper has deeply analyzed feasibility of the platform collaborative teaching and conducted further analysis of the application of cloud computing aided teaching platform on English major students teaching.

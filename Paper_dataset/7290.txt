DE cloud computing; cological agriculture; emote training; teaching system
AB With the continuous expansion of cloud computing in the application of distance training in ecological agriculture, how to establish a complete system to improve the effect of remote training of ecological agriculture, and to promote the further development of ecological agriculture is the important issues that need to be considered seriously in the current ecological agriculture and its training and education field. This paper mainly studies the major module design of the remote agriculture training teaching system based on cloud computing, and discusses the practical application of the remote training teaching system of ecological agriculture based on cloud computing, so as to contribute to the effective development of ecological agriculture remote training.

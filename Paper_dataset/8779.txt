DE Big data; data processing; system structure; distributed data storage
AB Under the background of big data, the theory and technology of computer data processing have received more and more attention. According to the characteristics of data processing mode, the computer data processing mode can be divided into four kinds: information virtualization technology, cloud computing, automated resource scheduling technology, data transmission technology. In this paper, the big data processing was discussed. The spatial data processing analysis model was established and applied to the urban industrial census. The data and decision support were provided for the development of urban industrialization and the structural optimization of urban economic industry.

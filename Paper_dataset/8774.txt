ID TEACHING BUSINESS ENGLISH
AB With the popularity of computer and network, demand on the storage and processing of mass data has increased. Hence, the application of cloud computing in data mining has attracted increasing attention. In this study, the design and implementation of a data mining platform based on cloud computing were analyzed and discussed. The concept and the specific research methods of this data mining platform were also described. Finally, the results of the research on the design and implementation of a data mining platform based on cloud computing were analyzed and discussed. This study provides a favorable research direction for the improvement and optimization of the specific algorithm design of a data mining platform based on cloud computing in the future.
